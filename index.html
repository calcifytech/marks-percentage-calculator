How to Calculate Percentage

  1. Divide obtained marks by maximum marks
  2. Multiply the result by 100
  3. Formula: (Obtained Marks ÷ Maximum Marks) × 100

Percentage Formula

Standard Calculation:

Percentage = (Scored Marks ÷ Test Maximum) × 100

Example: 175/200 marks = (175 ÷ 200) × 100 = 87.5%

Practical Examples

Case Study 1: 85/100 marks = (85/100) × 100 = 85%

Case Study 2: 147/150 marks = (147/150) × 100 = 98%

Class Performance Analysis

  1. Calculate individual student percentages
  2. Sum all percentages
  3. Divide total by number of students
  4. Example: 25 students averaging 78% = Class average 78%
